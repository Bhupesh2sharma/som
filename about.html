About Us
Our History
Mission & Vision

Nestled in the heart of the picturesque Chisopani, South & west Sikkim, Hotel Somras is more than just a place to stay; it's an experience waiting to be discovered. Our story is one of passion, hospitality, and a deep love for the beauty of South & west Sikkim.

Our Journey

Established with a vision to provide travelers with an immersive and authentic Sikkimese experience,Hotel somras has been welcoming guests since 2022. Our journey began with a simple yet profound idea: to create a tranquil haven amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Chisopani.

Our Commitment

AtHotel somras, our commitment is to ensure that every guest leaves with cherished memories. Our dedicated team of professionals goes above and beyond to provide warm hospitality, personalized service, and a sense of belonging. We are passionate about introducing our guests to the rich culture, natural wonders, and warm hospitality that defines South & west Sikkim.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the preferred choice for travelers seeking an authentic South & west Sikkim experience. We aspire to be known not just for our comfortable accommodations but also for our commitment to responsible tourism, community engagement, and preserving the natural beauty that makes South & west Sikkim so special.

Join us!

We invite you to be a part of the Hotel somras family and embark on a journey of discovery, relaxation, and cultural immersion. Experience South & west Sikkim through our eyes, and let us make your stay a truly unforgettable one.
