South Sikkim

South Sikkim is a hidden gem nestled in the eastern Himalayas, offering travelers a tranquil escape into nature's embrace. Known for its lush greenery, cascading waterfalls, and serene monasteries, this region is a paradise for nature enthusiasts and spiritual seekers alike.

West Sikkim

Nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, West Sikkim offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, rich culture, and serene spirituality. This enchanting region, less explored than its eastern counterpart, is a haven for those seeking an off-the-beaten-path experience.

Explore
East Sikkim

Nestled in the lap of the mighty Himalayas, East Sikkim is a picturesque and enchanting destination that offers a blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and adventure opportunities. Located in the northeastern part of India, this region is a must-visit for travelers seeking a unique and serene experience.

Our History
Mission & Vision

Nestled in the heart of the picturesque Chisopani, South & West Sikkim, Hotel Somras is more than just a place to stay; it's an experience waiting to be discovered. Our story is one of passion, hospitality, and a deep love for the beauty of Sikkim.

Our Journey

Established with a vision to provide travelers with an immersive and authentic Sikkimese experience, Hotel Somras has been welcoming guests since 2022. Our journey began with a simple yet profound idea: to create a tranquil haven amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Chisopani.

Our Commitment

At Hotel Somras, our commitment is to ensure that every guest leaves with cherished memories. Our dedicated team of professionals goes above and beyond to provide warm hospitality, personalized service, and a sense of belonging. We are passionate about introducing our guests to the rich culture, natural wonders, and warm hospitality that defines Sikkim.
